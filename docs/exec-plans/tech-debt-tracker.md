## Priority Levels

- **P0 — Critical:** Blocks development or causes production issues. Resolve immediately.
- **P1 — High:** Significant impact on velocity or quality. Resolve within current sprint.
- **P2 — Medium:** Causes friction but has workarounds. Schedule for next sprint.
- **P3 — Low:** Nice to fix, no immediate impact. Address when convenient.
